# Executive Dashboard Story

## Executive Summary

This Tableau Executive Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of retail business performance by combining key performance indicators with interactive visualizations. The dashboard enables leadership to monitor sales growth, profitability, regional performance, category profitability, and the relationship between discounts and profit.

## What Is Performing Well

The business generated approximately ₹217.02 million in total sales with an overall profit of ₹33.31 million and a profit margin of 15.35%. Sales remained relatively stable throughout the observed period with multiple months crossing ₹10 million in revenue. The South region generated the highest sales among all regions, indicating strong market performance. Technology products, particularly Copiers, Accessories, Phones, and Machines, contributed the highest profits.

## Underperforming Areas

Certain product sub-categories such as Paper, Binders, Storage, and Art generated comparatively low profits. Regional sales in the East and West remained below the leading South region. Scatter plot analysis also indicates that higher discounts generally correspond with lower profitability, suggesting reduced margins on heavily discounted orders.

## Business Risks

Heavy discounting may negatively impact overall profitability. Lower-performing product categories require evaluation to determine whether pricing, inventory, or marketing strategies should be revised. Regional differences also indicate potential market imbalance.

## Business Opportunities

The organization can increase revenue by strengthening successful product categories within Technology while expanding successful regional strategies into lower-performing regions. Discount policies can be optimized to improve margins without significantly affecting sales volume.

## Recommended Business Actions

- Optimize discount policies to improve profit margins.
- Expand successful regional sales strategies to underperforming regions.
- Increase marketing investment for high-profit technology products.
- Review pricing and inventory decisions for low-profit product categories.
- Continue monitoring monthly sales trends to identify seasonal demand.

## Dashboard Limitations

The dashboard focuses on historical sales data and does not include inventory availability, operational costs, customer lifetime value, or future forecasting.

## Suggested Next Analysis

Future dashboards can include customer retention analysis, sales forecasting, inventory optimization, geographic heat maps, and predictive analytics using machine learning models.
